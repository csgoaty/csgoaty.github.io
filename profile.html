This affected his social skills as he had not met other people for around 7 months. He grew up to be extremely clingy to me (which I dont mind) but I needed to train him to be more social. I used this video to help with that

It was a little challenging but eventually he became super socialable and everyone could play with this little cute ball of energy. I would say that buddy is my favourite dog mainly because of how close we became during that quarantine period as he always stayed with me to keep me company.
